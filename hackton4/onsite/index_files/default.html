Unified Login is Here

The RealPage® Unified Platform provides you with one login, from one location, for your RealPage products. Administrators will save hours spent managing users, and users will save time accessing and transitioning between RealPage products. Here's how you can request to migrate to Unified Platform.

1. Visit www.realpage.com/imready.

To learn more about migration and get started, visit the URL above, watch the video and fill out the form.

2. Get Up to Speed.

Make sure you watch the Unified Platform Migration Readiness videos available on the RealPage Product Learning Portal (or your EasyLMS®). Search for key term "migration readiness."

3. Answer the Call.

After receiving your migration request form (allow up to five business days), a RealPage Unified Platform Specialist will contact you to answer your migration questions, and guide you through the migration readiness tasks.

There are no orders required or additional RealPage costs to migrate to Unified Platform. When you are ready to migrate, RealPage will provide you with an implementation consultant and easy-to-use migration tool to make the transition to Unified Login seamless and easy. Simply, launch the app and start your user migration! We can't wait to save your company hours of time spent on user management, multiple logins and training.
